Lasagna

Description

This is a beautiful Italian Lasagna with layers of slow cooked Rag√π Bolognese and Besciamella cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan sauce
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
